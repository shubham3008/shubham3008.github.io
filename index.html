Welcome to my site.

I am a PhD student at the Centre for Machine Intelligence and Data Science (C-MInDS) department. Prior to this, I was enrolled in a five-year interdisciplinary dual degree (B.Tech + M.Tech) programme at C-MInDS under Prof. Sharat Chandran affiliated with the Department of Computer Science, IIT Bombay. My research interests broadly lie in the intersection of computer graphics, computer vision, and machine learning, with my master's dissertation topic being Simplifying BRDF Acquisition.

I have been fortunate to work with Prof. Pengtao Xie and Prof. Anupama Kowli during my bachelor's. I am also thankful to Prof. Aditya Sole for allowing me to visit and work on my dissertation at the Colourlab, Norwegian University of Science and Technology. Additionally, I had the valuable experience of interning under Prof. Paula Branco at the University of Ottawa as a recipient of the MITACS Globalink research award.

In my early college years, I gathered industry experience as a Data Science intern at Carnot Technologies and as an AI consultant at Qen Labs Inc. A summary of my past research work can be found here.
