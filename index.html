Our Work

The initiative aims at assessing the feasibility of using a mobile based application, START, for screening of neurodevelopmental disorders in children. The app is deployed on Android devices which can be used in low resource settings in Low Middle Income Countries (LMIC). The inititative has collected data for more than 100 children in the Delhi NCR region in India
