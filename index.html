Welcome to my site.

I am a master's student under Prof. Sharat Chandran affiliated with the Department of Computer Science, IIT Bombay. My research interests broadly lie in the intersection of deep learning and computer graphics with my thesis being in neural representation of bidirectional reflectance distribution function (BRDF).

I am enrolled in a five year interdisciplinary dual degree programme which started off with a bachelor's in Chemical Engineering and an auxiliary degree in Statistics. I have been fortunate to conduct research under Prof. Aditya Sole during my summer visit to Norwegian University of Science and Technology, prior to which I worked with Prof. Paula Branco from University of Ottawa as a MITACS Globalink research intern.

In my early college years I gathered industry experience as a Data Science intern at Carnot Technologies and as an AI consultant at Qen Labs Inc. A summary of my past research work can be found here.
